Cancun,Mexico

The most famous Mexican tourist destination in the world. Cancun owes its popularity to the beauty of its beaches, fun adventure parks, nearby Mayan archaeological sites and its hotel capacity. You don't even need to leave Cancun's hotel zone to enjoy the best beaches in the Caribbean. From the scenic tower you will have the most spectacular views of the city and in its surroundings you can dive in the best reefs of the sea. You will only have to board a boat in the hotel zone or Puerto Juarez to go to Cozumel or Isla Mujeres. Parks near Cancun such as Xplor, Xcaret and Xel-Ha offer the most exciting adventure sports in beautiful settings.
