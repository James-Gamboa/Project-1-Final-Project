Travel Destinies

Cancun, Mexico

The most famous Mexican tourist destination in the world. Cancun owes its popularity to the beauty of its beaches, fun adventure parks, nearby Mayan archaeological sites and its hotel capacity.

Tahiti-France

This French island territory in Polynesia is famous for its beautiful beaches ideal for diving, swimming and other sea sports.

Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

Punta Cana is so popular that its international airport handles more passengers than Santo Domingo's air terminal.

Rome, Italy

Rome receives annually more than 7 million foreign tourists who want to see the monuments of the former Roman Empire.

Maui, Hawaii, U.S.A.

Maui is the second largest island of Hawaii but the most popular among tourists, for its 50 km of paradisiacal beaches, lush jungles and magnificent golf courses

New Zealand

This oceanic archipelago has grown as a tourist destination for its volcanoes, Edenic beaches and cities with a high standard of living. Auckland is its most populated city with beautiful beaches, jungle coasts and charming bays.
